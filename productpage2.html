Product description

Alright, let's clear something up.

You think you've smelled frangipani before.

You haven't.

What most perfumes call frangipani? It's the airbrushed version - bright, sugary, tropical, and honestly... kind of forgettable.

Frangi Velvet is what happens when you meet the real thing.

We're talking true frangipani absolute, hand-sourced from Southern Thailand - buttery, floral, rich as silk, and surprisingly grounding. It doesn't float. It glows. And just when you think that glow has peaked, in comes the wild Western Australian Sandalwood - and trust us, this one has attitude.

It's creamy, yes, but also textured, full of grain and soul. It doesn't just smooth things out - it gives the whole scent depth, direction, and that addictive, velvety feel that clings to skin like warmth after the sun sets.

A lift of rose absolute and geranium adds light. Then the base gets serious: patchouli, guaiacwood, a hint of Thai oud, and cedarwood create a soft, earthy hum that anchors the floral heart without muting it.

This isn't a loud fragrance. It's a slow-burn stunner.

It won't try to impress you. It will stay with you - and maybe even haunt you a little.
